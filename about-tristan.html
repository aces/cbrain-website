Tristan Glatard, PhD.

Assistant Professor at the Department of Computer-Science and Software Engineering at at Concordia University

Dr. Glatard is an Assistant Professor at the Department of Computer-Science and Software Engineering at at Concordia University, head of the Big Data Infrastructures for Neuroinformatics lab, and member of the PERFORM centre and Data Science Research Centre at Concordia University in Montreal, and Adjunct Professor at the School of Computer-Science at McGill. Before, he was a Researcher at the French National Centre for Scientific Research. He is the progenitor of Boutiques, a flexible framework to integrate command-line applications in computing platforms and the CARMIN common web API for remote pipeline execution.
