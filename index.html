CBRAIN is an infrastructure that connects researchers from all walks of science and their data to high performance and cloud computing in a easy to manage, reproducible, and user-friendly manner. CBRAIN focuses on:

Accessibility

Ability to work across multiple machines and filesystems.

Efficiency

Quickly moving compute and data behind the scenes.

Reproducibility

Strict standards and pipelines allow for reproducible results.

Anyone can use CBRAIN by signing up for an account on our publicly available portal, or download the source hosted at Github to deploy CBRAIN for yourself.
